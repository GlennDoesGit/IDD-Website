Go Zenith

Transferring to Zenith Has Never Been Easier

Aura Transfer App

Start by downloading the Zenith app on your previous phone.

To boost the speed of your transfer, just use a USB‑C cable to connect the two devices.

It securely transfers the stuff you care about most — your contacts, messages, WhatsApp content, photos, videos, email accounts, favourite apps, call history and more — from your previous phone to your Aura.

Manual Transfer

Data transfer can also be done manually by connecting both devices with a compatible wire.
